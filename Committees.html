General Assemblies

ECOWAS

On 26 July 2023, Niger's civilian government was deposed. ECOWAS, a collection of West African countries stand at the ready, prepared to invade to topple the military junta. But Niger is not alone, forming an alliance of with other junta led countries in the Sahel. The two alliances are locked into a staring match. Will the subcontinent be dragged into another African World War or will peace and diplomacy prevail?

Specialized Agencies

Gulf War (UNSC)

On August 2, 1990, Iraq invaded Kuwait for control over their oil fields, igniting a global crisis that compelled the UN to confront this violation of international peace with a military response. Delegates will first discuss Operation Desert Shield, the prelude to the invasion, debating on the enforcement of sanctions on Iraq, the mounting threat of conflict escalation and the increase of coalition forces to defend the region. Once Iraq refuses to withdraw, delegates will then move on to Operation Desert Storm, the coalition response to force Iraq’s compliance. Members of the committee will focus on the balance between international law and the ethics of intervention, the urgent need for the protection of civilians, planning post-conflict recovery and the potential UN sanctions to be placed on Iraq for its hostile invasion.

Pokemon

3 months have passed since Team Flare’s attack on the Kalos region. Fights had broken out and needed the entire team of gym leaders of Kalos to wrap things up, fires burned forests, buildings collapsed, a new species of pokemon was discovered, and mega evolution energy went crazy. Although the city has been fixed for the most part, Team Flare’s impact, Zygard's discovery, and Mega Evolution still remain. News about the situation has reached other regions like Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh, Unova, Alola, Galar, and Paldea. The Pokémon society has been changed…forever.

Crisis Committees

Transformers

In a war-torn Cybertron divided between Autobots and Decepticons, Energon is running out and desperation is rewriting the rules. When Ultra Magnus—the Autobots’ top military commander—is captured and taken to a Decepticon fortress for brutal experimentation under Shockwave, the Autobots are forced into a high-risk rescue mission against Megatron’s kill-on-sight orders. At the same time, both factions scramble for survival through espionage, raids, and rumors of hidden Titans buried beneath Iacon—while whispers of the Allspark surface as the ultimate prize. Which of the two faction will prevail?

Squid Game

On an island off the coast of South Korea, financially disadvantages contestants risk their lives to participate in a series of deadly children’s games for a prize of 45.6M Won. As the game progresses, delegates must confront the morality of these games; is it really okay to run them—at the cost of the lives of real people—for the purpose of entertaining the rich and wealthy? How will players and organizers decide to move forward—will they expose, silence, or expand the games?

Chechen War

The First Chechen War has ended in a decisive victory for the Chechens. Yet Russia sits on the doorsteps, waiting for a moment of weakness. Whatever remains is in ruins, and the young nation has no friends in this time of need. Grozny must be rebuilt before Russia comes back for a vengeance.
